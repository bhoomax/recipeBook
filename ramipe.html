Ramen

Japanese

Japanese noodle soup featuring wheat noodles served in a savory broth, often with toppings like sliced pork, boiled egg, green onions, and nori (seaweed).

Ingredients

  • Wheat noodles
  • savory broth
  • boiled egg
  • nori (seaweed)

Cooking Instructions

Boil noodles until tender, then drain and rinse. Simmer broth with desired seasonings. Serve noodles in broth, topped with sliced pork, egg, green onions, and nori.
